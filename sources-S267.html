    Mrs. Louie Miner Seely, 52, died of a heart ailment Monday at the home of a sister, Mrs. Arthur Snow.

    A native of Fairview, Mrs. Seely was born October 11, 1880, a daughter of Mormon and Emeline Curtis Miner.

    After her marriage to Earl H. Seely, a prominent livestock grower of Mt. Pleasant, she moved to Mt. Pleasant, where she lived until coming to Salt Lake City three years ago. her husband died in 1930.

    Surviving are three sons, John G., Earl L. and Calvin M. Seely of Salt Lake City; two daughters, Mrs. W. E. Watson of Pocetello, Idaho; Miss Marjorie L. Seely of Salt Lake City; three grandchildren; a sister, Mrs. Arthur E. Snow of Salt Lake City and five brothers, A. U. and Lester Miner of Fairview; Frank Miner of Salt Lake City, and Erueal L. Miner of Price and George D. Miner of Provo Bench.

    Salt Lake Tribune January 19, 1942
