    Evaline Marie Miner Jones, age 86, passed away on April 9, 2000 in Shreveport, Louisiana.

    Born June 23, 1913 in Ault, Colorado to DeMaus and Mayree Arps Miner.

    Married Carl L. Jones on January 27, 1935.

    Former resident of Salt Lake City.

    Survived by husband, Shreveport, Louisiana; children, Lon L. (Beverly) Jones and Jerry Dee (Chris) Jones; and seven grandchildren.

    Graveside service will be held at 12:00 p.m. on Thursday at Elysian Burial Gardens.
    Published in the Deseret News on April 11, 2000.
