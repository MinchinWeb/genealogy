    Beloved Son of Mormon and Pheobe Emaline Curtis Miner, Beloved Husband of Lillie Elizabeth Anderson Miner. High Priest and Member of the Wells Ward and Temple Worker for 20 years. Funeral Services were held at the Wells Ward Chapel on Tuesday April 7, 1965 at 1990 South 5th East at 12:15 PM. A Viewing was held at 255-2nd East on Monday April 6, 1965 From 6-8 PM and One Hour Prior To Services At The Church. A Graveside Service took place following the Funeral Services. Rest In Peace. (bio by: Ryan D. Curtis)
