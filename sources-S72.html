  • BRUMWELL-CHRISTENSEN,
    Sally Marie
    March 5, 1947 - January 14, 2012
    It is with great sadness that we announce the passing of our wife, mother, sister, aunt and friend, Sally Marie Brumwell-Christensen. Family, love and laughter defined Sally's life. She was born in Esterhazy, SK to Charles and Mary Bedwell. Sally was a skilled artisan, an avid gardener and a devoted grandmother. Sally leaves behind Gord, the love of her life and soul-mate; her children, Andrea (Ekke), Jen and Rob (Stacy); brothers, Roger (Lois), Chris (Rose) and Greg (Terry); sisters Pat and Jacquie (Brad); and grandchildren, Jordan, Madis, Caylin, Natan, Noah, Hale, Brandon, Dominic and Kaira. Sally will also be missed by Gord's family, her nieces and nephews and many good friends. Funeral Services will be held at Deer Park United Church (77 Deer Point Road S.E.) on Friday, January 20, 2012 at 2:00 p.m. Forward condolences through www.mcinnisandholloway.com . Memorial donations can be made to the Canadian Tire Jump Start Charity. We would like to thank Dr. Hall, Dr. Morris, Home Care Nurses and all the amazing doctors, nurses and staff at the Tom Baker Centre. In living memory of Sally Brumwell-Christensen, a tree will be planted at Fish Creek Provincial Park.



    Published in The Calgary Herald from Jan. 18 to Jan. 19, 2012
