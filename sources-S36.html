    I am looking to trace my grandmother's origins in Ireland. She was kathleen Susan Bunbury and married a Reverend Robert Miller, like you a Trinity man. Her brother was George Bunbury Both were orphaned her father was an Indian Army officer. George was apprenticed at the age of 16 to Canada where he prospered. Her husband died early and in the early 1920's she came to England. The family were friend with the Frenches, although better she thought. Best wishes, David Prout
