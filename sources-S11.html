    The James Wells family farmed at Dennington, James and his wife Ann are buried together at buried at the Rendham Independent Chapel.

    The inscription on their burial marker reads:
    Memory of/
    ANN WELLS/ died 13 Feb 1799/ Aged 65 years
    Jas WELLS/ Died 26 July/ 1796 Aged 61 years

    Dangers stand thick thro all the ground, To push us to the Tomb/
    And fierce diseases wait around/
    to hurry mortals home/
    Then what your thoughts design to do/
    Your hands with all their might persue/
    Since no device or work is found/
    Nor faith nor hope beneath the ground
