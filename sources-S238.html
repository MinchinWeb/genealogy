    Earl Martin Miner was born December 3, 1889 in Murray, Utah to Martin Mormon Miner and Evelyn Brown.

    He married Thelma Brown on July 19, 1916 in Manti, Utah. They had six children. Earl was a carpenter in the Los Angeles area for several years. He was a member of the Democratic Party.

    Earl passed away on Wednesday, May 30, 1973 in San Gabriel, California at the age of 83 years old. He resided at 1142 East 75th Street in Florence, California at the time of his death.

    Earl was laid to rest at the Rose Hills Memorial Park in Whittier, California.
