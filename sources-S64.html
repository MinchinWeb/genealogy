  • NOTES:
    - No church baptism records for this family have survived.
    - Children 1, 3, 4, 6 and 7 are listed as beneficiaries in the 1900 will of Hamilton Sinnamon. Martha Jane is recorded as Jane.
    - Child 5 (Robert) is not referenced but is a witness to the will
    - Birth date for son Henry is as established in 1900 US National Census (location Philadelphia PA)
    - Birth dates for others are estimated from 1901 and 1911 Irish Census records except where corrected by data from certified ages at death. MI in Pomeroy Crosdernot cemetery contains the only reference to Sarah who died young.
    - Nominal birth order as shown is determined by certified ages at death - may be reversed for children 6 and 7?
    - Most ages listed in the 1901 census appear to be underestimated.

About Gigatrees 4.0.5

Gigatrees is a free, Windows 64 utility written by Tim Forsythe (© 2016).

It can be used to validate a GEDCOM file, generate standalone genealogy related reports, or build an entire family tree website.

Gigatrees provides many unique features, including support for the Genealogy Proof Standard, data consistency checking, census table reports, population distribution heatmaps, location maps, generation lists, kinship lines, full language translations and more.
